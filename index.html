About me

I am from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I am currently finishing my degree in Industrial Design at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA). I have several years of experience working in design. The last two years I got into the world of software and digital products.

I provide solutions for your project; architecture diagrams, UI/UX, prototyping, web development and brand identity. I am also dedicated to organization and planning, agile work methodologies, team management and communication with clients.

In my spare time I like to paint, cook and watch movies. I regard myself as a comitted, orderly and creative person as well as a responsible and capable one with strong inclination towards teamwork.
