About me

I'm a designer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I'm 25 years old.

I'm currently working as Product Owner at VreadyNow; an emerging technology company that provides Virtual and Augmented Reality application software and services. In addition, I am studying Industrial Design at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA).

I provide solutions for digital projects: Organization and planning, agile work methodologies like Scrum, team management, product design, user analysis and experience, customer communication, content creation and web development.

I regard myself as a comitted, orderly and creative person as well as a responsible and capable one with strong inclination towards teamwork. In my free time I love painting, drawing, reading and spent time with my family and friends.
